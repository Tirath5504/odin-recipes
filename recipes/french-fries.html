French Fries

Description

French fries, also known as "fries" or "chips," are a popular and beloved side dish enjoyed worldwide. Made from freshly cut potatoes, they are typically deep-fried until golden and crispy on the outside, while remaining tender and fluffy on the inside. The perfect French fry strikes a balance between crispy texture and comforting taste. Often seasoned with salt, they can be served on their own or paired with various condiments like ketchup, mayonnaise, or gravy. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack or a delightful accompaniment to burgers and sandwiches, French fries have become an iconic and comforting indulgence for people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

    • Black Pepper
    • Paprika
  • Ketchup

Steps
